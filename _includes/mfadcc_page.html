Impact of Real-time Map Feedback on Coordinated Image Sampling for 3D Reconstruction

Muhammad Hanif1, Takumi Sumino1, Kuniaki Uto2, Daisuke Ichihashi3, Kelvin Cheng3, Takeshi Hatanaka1
1Institute of Science Tokyo, 2Wise SSS Tokyo Tech, 3Rakuten Mobile Inc.
Paper Video

Abstract

Efficient image sampling from diverse viewing angles is crucial for high-quality 3D map reconstruction, and coverage control provides a promising solution to this mission. Moreover, with the recent advancements in real-time 3D reconstruction algorithms, it is now possible to iteratively reconstruct 3D maps in real time, providing immediate map feedback to robot motion control. In this paper, we propose a novel coordinated image sampling algorithm that leverages real-time map feedback to enhance the quality of the reconstructed 3D model.
